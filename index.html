I love you with all my heart. Completely. Deeply. Honestly. And I cannot wait for the day when distance is no longer part of our story — when I get to wake up next to you, build a home with you, and live life together, side by side.

Happy 6 months, my love.
Happy first Valentine’s.
To us — and to forever.
